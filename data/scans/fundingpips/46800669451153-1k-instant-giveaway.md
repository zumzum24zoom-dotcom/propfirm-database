Maximum Loss Limit

**Maximum Loss Limit: 5%**(of the Instant Giveaway Account Size)

The Maximum Loss Limit is the total amount your equity or balance cannot go below. Violating this rule is a hard breach and will lead to the closure of your account.

**Example:** For a $1,000 account, your equity or balance cannot drop to **$950**.

Daily Loss Limit

**Daily Loss Limit: 5%**(of the higher value between your starting equity or balance)

The Daily Loss Limit is the amount you are allowed to lose in a single day. The higher value between your starting equity or balance is used. Violating this rule is a hard breach and will lead to closure of your account.

- **Example 1 (Higher Equity):** Starting with a $1,050 balance and $1,070 equity, the 5% limit is based on equity. Your equity cannot fall to **$1,016.50** that day.
- **Example 2 (Higher Balance):** Starting with a $1,000 balance and $990 equity, the 5% limit is based on balance. Your equity cannot fall to **$950.00** that day.

Important

- The equity for the day (floating profit/loss plus closed positions) must not hit the Daily Loss limit.
- The Daily Loss Limit resets at **00:00 Platform Time (UTC+3)**.
- You can always check the reset timer on your FundingPips dashboard.

How does this work? ↓

On Demand Rewards (50% Reward Split)

You can request a reward with a 50% reward split any time once the minimum profit amount is met. Each reward splits 50/50 between two channels:

25% of profit

Cash

Sent via your selected reward method: card, crypto, Rise, or Tradin transfer.

25% of profit

Tradin Credit

Auto-transferred to your Tradin account. Usable for trading on Tradin only.

**Example:** $100 in profit becomes a $50 reward, split as $25 cash + $25 Tradin Credit.

**About Tradin Credit:** Tradin Credit lands in the Credit slot on your Tradin account and is **permanently non-withdrawable and non-transferable**. It is usable for trading only and does not convert to Balance. This is distinct from the Tradin Bonus (the 30% bonus on FP-to-Tradin reward transfers), which also lands as Credit but converts to Balance via cashback on opened lots.

The 25% Tradin Credit auto-transfer applies only in **Tradin-supported countries**. In unsupported countries, the auto-transfer does not apply.

There is a **20-lot limit** per click/trade. The platform will not allow higher lot sizes in one click. This is a per-trade limitation that protects against extreme market conditions at higher volumes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify to receive a 1K Instant Account?

The 1K Instant Account is a giveaway offered periodically by FundingPips. Eligibility and entry requirements are announced when each giveaway runs. Keep an eye on your dashboard and FundingPips communications for when the next one opens.

Can I take the full 1K reward as cash, or does part of it always go to Tradin?

Part of it always goes to Tradin. Each 1K reward splits 50/50: 25% of your profit is sent as cash via your chosen reward method (card, crypto, Rise, or Tradin transfer), and 25% lands on your Tradin account as Tradin Credit. Tradin Credit is usable for trading on Tradin only; it is non-withdrawable and non-transferable. The split applies only in Tradin-supported countries; you cannot opt out of it on this account type.

What happens if my country doesn't support Tradin?

The 25% Tradin Credit auto-transfer applies only in Tradin-supported countries. If Tradin is not supported in your country, the auto-transfer does not apply and the remaining reward is sent through your selected reward method.

Does a momentary equity dip count as a breach, even if it recovers?

Yes. Even a brief or momentary drop in equity below the allowed threshold is considered a breach, regardless of whether the account recovers afterward. The breach is confirmed the moment the threshold is touched, including through open positions that push your equity below the limit.

Can I trade crypto on the 1K Instant Account?

No. Crypto is excluded from the 1K Instant Account. Only the instrument groups in the leverage table (Forex Majors, Forex Exotics, Metals, Indices, and Oil) are available on this account type.

Can I use a trade copier or Expert Advisor on the 1K Instant Account?

Yes. Third-party EAs and trade copiers are fully permitted on the 1K Instant Account, including for full automation. This is the exception to the rule on all other account types. Proof of ownership is not required.

Can I get a reset if my 1K Instant Account is breached?

No. Evaluation Resets are only available when you fail Phase 1 or Phase 2 of a standard evaluation (1 Step, 2 Step Standard, or 2 Step Pro). A breached 1K Instant Account does not trigger a reset option.

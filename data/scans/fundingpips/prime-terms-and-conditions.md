Smart Maximum Loss Limit: The Prime Account is subject to a Smart Maximum Loss Limit, the amount of which is determined by the evaluation type completed by the Customer. The Smart Maximum Loss Limit trails the highest balance recorded by the account on a one-to-one basis until it reaches the corresponding locked level, after which it is fixed. The initial and locked levels are:

- 2-Step: initial limit of ten percent (10%), locked limit of seven percent (7%);

- 2-Step Pro: initial limit of six percent (6%), locked limit of four percent (4%);

- 1-Step: initial limit of six percent (6%), locked limit of four percent (4%);

- Zero: initial limit of five percent (5%), locked limit of three percent (3%).


All percentages are calculated based on the account size.

*Example (USD 100,000 Prime Account):*

- *2-Step: the Smart Maximum Loss Limit begins at USD 90,000, trails upward as the account records new highest balances, and is fixed at USD 93,000 once the account balance reaches USD 103,000;*

- *2-Step Pro / 1-Step: the Smart Maximum Loss Limit begins at USD 94,000 and is fixed at USD 96,000 once the account balance reaches USD 102,000;*

- *Zero: the Smart Maximum Loss Limit begins at USD 95,000 and is fixed at USD 97,000 once the account balance reaches USD 102,000.*


Specifically:

1.  The reference balance for the Smart Maximum Loss Limit is the account balance at the time of the most recent scaling event, or the initial Prime Account balance if no scaling has occurred;

2.  The Smart Maximum Loss Limit applies to both open and closed positions at all times;

3.  Should the account balance or equity reach or breach the Smart Maximum Loss Limit, the Prime Account will be immediately suspended, and this Agreement will automatically terminate in accordance with clause ‎10;
